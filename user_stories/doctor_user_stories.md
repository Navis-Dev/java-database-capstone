**Title: Doctor Management**
_As a doctor, I want to manage my schedule and patient information, so that I can provide effective healthcare._

**Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Log into the portal to manage your appointments
2. Log out of the portal to protect my data
3. View my appointment calendar to stay organized
4. Mark your unavailability to inform patients only the available slots
5. Update your profile with specialization and contact information so that patients have up-to-date information
6. View the patient details for upcoming appointments so that I can be prepared

**Priority:** High
**Story Points:** 6
**Notes:**
- The doctor can log into the portal to manage their appointments and view their schedule.
- The doctor can mark their unavailability to inform patients about the available slots.
